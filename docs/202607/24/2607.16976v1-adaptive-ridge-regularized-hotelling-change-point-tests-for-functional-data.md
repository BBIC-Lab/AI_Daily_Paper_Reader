## 摘要
我们提出了一个统一的岭正则化霍特林框架，用于检测和定位函数型时间序列中的均值变点。通过递增的基展开将函数型观测转化为高维得分向量，其长期协方差由边缘校正的差分方法估计。岭正则化在谱衰减下稳定了推断。显式的局部功效公式表明，最大化功效的岭参数取决于未知的变化谱方向。因此，我们通过柯西变换将一族岭CUSUM统计量组合起来，并直接从它们的联合加权桥极限进行标定。对于多变点，我们将局部最大岭统计量嵌入到Wild二叉分割过程中，随后进行局部细化。在温和条件下，我们建立了所提检验的有效性、局部功效、一致性和定位性质。在多变点情形下，该过程一致地恢复变点数量并均匀估计其位置。该框架适应弱相依和非高斯函数型误差。模拟和两个实证应用展示了所提方法良好的有限样本表现。

## Abstract
We propose a unified ridge-regularized Hotelling framework for detecting and locating mean changes in functional time series. A growing basis expansion converts the functional observations into high-dimensional score vectors. Their long-run covariance is estimated by an edge-corrected difference-based procedure. Ridge regularization stabilizes inference under spectral decay. An explicit local-power formula shows that the power-maximizing ridge depends on the unknown spectral orientation of the change. We therefore combine a family of ridge CUSUM statistics by a Cauchy transform and calibrate the aggregate directly from their joint weighted-bridge limit. For multiple changes, we embed local maximum-ridge statistics in a wild binary segmentation procedure, followed by local refinement. Under mild conditions, we establish the validity, local power, consistency, and localization properties of the proposed tests. In the multiple-change setting, the procedure consistently recovers the number of changes and uniformly estimates their locations. The framework accommodates weak dependence and non-Gaussian functional errors. Simulations and two empirical applications demonstrate the favorable finite-sample performance of the proposed methods.
