## 摘要
似然方法是霍克斯过程推断的标准方法，而计算需求较低的方法大多独立发展。我们证明最小二乘、Takács--Fiksel以及相关的矩估计量构成了一类基于补偿器的估计方程，其中似然得分作为有效基准。对于具有紧记忆、非线性正链接和允许抑制的有符号核的固定维多元霍克斯过程，任何适当正则的固定滞后窗的可预测泛函，在与 $\text{d} N-λ\,\text{d} t$ 积分时，都会给出一个无偏估计方程。在通常的正则性、识别性和秩条件下，基于每个可接受有限库的估计量都能达到一致的高概率和逐点几乎必定的 $\mathcal O(\sqrt{\log(T)/T})$ 收敛速度，具有 Godambe 协方差的渐近正态性，并允许可行的两步最优加权。投影恒等式量化了每个库在可预测张成空间之外的得分信息所带来的精确效率损失；一个两点界表明根-$T$ 尺度无法一致地改进。尽管紧记忆局部化了强度而非平稳律，指数遗忘给出了 Bernstein 型集中不等式，并将理论推广到具有对数预热期的非平稳起始情况。在此范围内，补偿器类对于有限库比较是穷尽的：它包含得分，为可接受库提供共同保证，并精确量化它们的效率差距。

## Abstract
Likelihood is standard for Hawkes-process inference, while less computationally demanding methods have largely developed separately. We show that least squares, Takács--Fiksel, and related moment-based estimators form a single class of compensator-based estimating equations, with the likelihood score as the efficient benchmark. For fixed-dimensional multivariate Hawkes processes with compact memory, nonlinear positive links, and signed kernels allowing inhibition, every suitably regular predictable functional of a fixed lag window yields an unbiased estimating equation when integrated against $\text{d} N-λ\,\text{d} t$. Under common regularity, identification, and rank conditions, estimators based on every admissible finite library achieve uniform high-probability and pointwise almost-sure $\mathcal O(\sqrt{\log(T)/T})$ rates, asymptotic normality with Godambe covariance, and admit feasible two-step optimal weighting. A projection identity quantifies each library's exact efficiency loss as the score information outside its predictable span; a two-point bound shows the root-$T$ scale cannot be improved uniformly. Although compact memory localizes the intensity rather than the stationary law, exponential forgetting yields Bernstein-type concentration and transfers the theory to nonstationary starts after a logarithmic burn-in. Within this scope, the compensator class is exhaustive for finite-library comparisons: it contains the score, gives admissible libraries common guarantees, and quantifies their efficiency gaps exactly.
